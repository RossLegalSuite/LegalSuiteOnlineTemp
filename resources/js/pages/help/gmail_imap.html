Step 1: Check that IMAP is turned on

  1. If you use Gmail on this device, open your Gmail app by clicking here.
  2. Otherwise open your Gmail app on a device from which you send and receive email.
  3. Once you have opened Gmail, in the top right, click Settings and then See all settings.
  4. Click the Forwarding and POP/IMAP tab.
  5. In the "IMAP access" section, select Enable IMAP.
  6. Click Save Changes.

Step 2: Allow "Less Secure Apps" to access your Gmail

Gmail restricts access from third party apps by default.

To allow Ettorney to access your email, click here to go to your Google account settings and make sure "Allow less secure apps" is set to "ON"

Step 3: Gmail settings

Use the following settings to setup the Incoming and Outgoing servers in Ettorney.

Outgoing (SMTP) Server

Server: smtp.gmail.com

Port: 587

Encryption: TLS

Authentication: Yes
Incoming (IMAP) Server

Server: imap.gmail.com

Port: 993

Encryption: SSL
